Core Features

TAO is a decentralized borrowing protocol offering interest-free liquidity against collateral in Ether. By utilizing a novel liquidation mechanism and leveraging an algorithmic monetary policy it can offer unprecedented benefits for borrowers.

0% Interest Rate

As a borrower, there’s no need to worry about constantly accruing debt

110% Collateralization Ratio

The highly efficient liquidation mechanism enables you to get the most liquidity for your SOL.

Directly Redeemable

tUSD can be redeemed at face value for the underlying collateral at any time

Governance Free

All operations are algorithmic and fully automated, and protocol parameters are set at time of contract deployment.

Robust & Stable

tUSD pegged to the US Dollar, 1 tUSD = 1 USD

Incentives for Stakers

The system captures the revenue from the borrowing and redemption fees and pays it out on a pro rata basis to the stakers of the TAO token.
